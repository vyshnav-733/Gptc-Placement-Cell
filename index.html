VISION

Excel as a center of skill education moulding professionals who sincerely strive for the betterment of society

MISSION

To impart state of the art knowledge and skill to the graduate and moulding them to be competent, committed and responsible for the wellbeing of society

Government Polytechnic College, Perumbavoor always had stood in the forefront in serving the societ as a technical institution providing quality skill education for the last 5 decades. The institution had contributed very much to the development of the locality as most of the alumni are employed with key role in technical departments of central, state and public sector establishments. The institute had contributed very much to the establishment of Perumbavoor as hub for plywood industry in the state. Established in the year 1959 as Junior Technical School and later on in 1994 upgraded to Govt. Polytechnic College under Department of Technical Education, Government of Kerala and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. With a built up area of over two hundred thousand square feet is situated in a sprawling campus of 6.93 acres, nestled among lush greenery, of Koovapady Panchayath, in middle of Ernakulum District. Situated at the middle of both industrial and tourist areas, the college provides a pleasant atmosphere for the students and is well connected. The infrastructure facilities are consistently being upgraded.

Various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities are organised for the development of skills in students. Every branch organises seminars by resource persons from outside the campus under the Visiting Faculty Programme. These sessions give students awareness about the latest trends in the fields of their studies and help to get a taste of the emerging technologies.The finishing school programme enhances the employability of the students by improving their communication skills and developing their personality. Students get guidance and service of the placement cell to know about better employment opportunities and to get placement according to their interests. The Industry- Institute Interaction Programmes for students are intended to give them an exposure outside the curriculum and campus. At present the college is a known name among the 43 Government Polytechnics in the state of Kerala. It functions under the department of technical education.
